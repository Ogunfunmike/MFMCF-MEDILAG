About Us

Our History

MFMCF MEDILAG was established to create a vibrant community of medical students dedicated to spiritual growth and academic excellence. We are a family of believers committed to raising prayer champions and medical professionals who will impact their world for Christ.

Our Vision

To raise a generation of medical professionals who are spiritually sound, academically excellent, and socially relevant.

Our Mission

Creating an enabling environment for medical students to know God, grow in faith, and excel in their studies through prayer, fellowship, and mutual support.

Academics & Spirituality

Study Tips for Medical Students

Time Management

Create a study schedule that balances academics, fellowship, and rest. Use the Pomodoro technique: 25 mins study, 5 mins break.

Study Groups

Form small study groups within the fellowship. Iron sharpens iron!

Pray Before Studying

Commit your studies to God. Pray for understanding and retention.

Active Recall

Practice active recall techniques. Test yourself regularly on what you've learned.

Academic Devotionals

Trusting God for Understanding

"If any of you lacks wisdom, let him ask of God, who gives to all liberally..." - James 1:5

As medical students, the volume of knowledge can be overwhelming. But remember, the same God who created the human body is willing to give you wisdom. Start each study session with prayer.

Excellence for God's Glory

"Whatever you do, work at it with all your heart, as working for the Lord..." - Colossians 3:23

Your studies are not just for grades or a degree. You're preparing to be a vessel of healing. Study with excellence as unto the Lord.

Rest in God's Peace

"Come to me, all you who are weary and burdened, and I will give you rest." - Matthew 11:28

During exam stress, take time to rest in God's presence. He is your peace and strength.
